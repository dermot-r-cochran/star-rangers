# Codex: Exploration Budget (EB)

The old **AI Usage Credits (AUC)** ledger counted machine-assisted thought as if thought were fuel. Useful, but narrow.

The modern doctrine is broader:

> Every engine of discovery spends order to buy possibility.

AUC remains in force as one instrument inside the larger **Exploration Budget (EB)** framework.

## Layered Entropy Doctrine

### Layer 1 — Mechanism

**entropy → variability → risk**

Generative systems require a minimum degree of outcome variability. Increased variability widens the distribution of reachable states, including undesirable ones. Hence, some level of risk is inherent to generative capacity.

### Layer 2 — Constraint

What level of variability is required?

Constrained generation narrows state-space but does not eliminate it. The question is not whether variability can be removed, but where the minimum viable variability lies for truthful, adaptive, and creative function.

### Layer 3 — Optimization

Can risk be reduced without losing capability?

Systems can reduce harmful output mass through architecture, incentives, evaluation, and governance. But reductions that collapse exploratory capacity can destroy the value function itself.

### Layer 4 — Evaluation

What harms are structurally required vs reducible?

This is a measurement problem and an ethics problem. We evaluate whether observed harm belongs to the irreducible floor of exploration or to preventable design failure.

## Entropy Accounting

\[
H_{total} = H_{necessary} + H_{contingent}
\]

- **Necessary entropy**: irreducible uncertainty required for learning, adaptation, and creative discovery.
- **Contingent entropy**: avoidable uncertainty introduced by poor incentives, weak controls, and negligent design.

In systems with generative capacity, the range of possible outcomes expands, increasing the probability of undesirable states. Therefore, some risk of undesirable outcomes is structurally unavoidable.

Doctrine does not seek zero entropy. Doctrine seeks the minimum contingent entropy compatible with mission capability.
