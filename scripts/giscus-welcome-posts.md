# Giscus welcome/starter posts — draft

Target repo: `Star-Rangers/sciencefiction-site-comments` (GitHub Discussions
backing the site's giscus widget — see `src/_data/giscus.js` and
README.md's "Discussion forum (giscus)" section).

Scope: one starter post per **open, community-facing** category only —
**General**, **Q&A**, **Theories & Predictions**, **Fan Creations**. The other
four categories (**Characters**, **Lore & Worldbuilding**, **Episodes
Discussion**, **Journal**) are locked to "Announcement" format so that only
the giscus app creates a discussion there, one per real page, the first time
someone comments on it — seeding those manually would create an orphan
discussion not tied to any actual page. **Announcements** is the maintainer's
own news feed, not a spot for a sample/community post, so it's skipped too.

Each post below is meant to be created as a new Discussion in the named
category, using the given title and body. Post author: dermot-r-cochran
(site owner), each body self-identifies as a sample comment generated by
Claude to seed the thread.

---

## Category: General

**Title:** Welcome — say hello

**Body:**

Welcome to the Star Rangers discussion board! This is the spot for anything
that doesn't fit neatly into Characters, Lore, Episodes, or Journal comments
below — general chat about the story, the site, or just introducing
yourself.

A few things that might help:

- Character, lore, glossary, codex, and chapter/scene pages each have their
  own comment thread attached directly to that page — if your comment is
  about one specific piece of content, it'll find a more engaged audience
  there.
- Have a theory about where the story's going? There's a dedicated
  **Theories & Predictions** category for that.
- Got a question about how something works, in-universe or out? Try **Q&A**.
- Made something inspired by Star Rangers? **Fan Creations** is the place to
  share a link and talk about it.

Feel free to introduce yourself below — what drew you to the Five Layers?

## Category: Q&A

**Title:** Ask your questions here

**Body:**

This category is for questions — about the story, the setting, how the site
works, anything you're curious about and want a direct answer to rather than
open-ended discussion.

If your question is about a specific character, location, or piece of lore,
check whether that page already has an answer in its own comment thread
first. Otherwise, ask away here. No question is too small, and "what does
this term mean" is exactly what the Glossary and Codex pages (and this
category) are for.

## Category: Theories & Predictions

**Title:** Share your theories

**Body:**

Got a hunch about where a thread is heading, what a Concordant is really
hiding, or how two characters' arcs are going to collide? This is the place
for it.

Speculation and open questions are welcome here — spoiler-light or
spoiler-heavy, just make it clear which one you're posting so other readers
can choose what to read. Half the fun of a multi-POV, multi-timeline story
is arguing about what's actually going on before it's confirmed.

## Category: Fan Creations

**Title:** Share your fan creations

**Body:**

Made art, music, a playlist, a map, or anything else inspired by Star
Rangers? Share a link and tell us about it here.

Note that this category is for links and discussion, not hosting: full fan
fiction belongs on [AO3](https://archiveofourown.org/) or
[Wattpad](https://www.wattpad.com/) rather than in this repo or its
Discussions (see the About page's "Fan fiction" section) — but a link to
your work, along with whatever you want to say about it, is very welcome
here.
