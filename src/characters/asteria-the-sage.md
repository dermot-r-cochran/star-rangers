Some officers retire from the Star Rangers into memoirs, some into consultancy, and a few into silence. Asteria retired into a chapter house.

**Asteria the Sage** is a retired Star Captain of the Star Rangers. She withdrew to a quiet planet after her service and now leads its local [Fellowship of Light](/star-rangers/lore/fellowship-of-light/) chapter — the honorific is the chapter's, not the Rangers', and she is said to tolerate rather than encourage it.

A career spent commanding a Ranger vessel through boundary zones leaves a person with firsthand working knowledge of things most people only meet as doctrine, which is why visitors still find their way to her with questions about the [Cosmic Cascade](/star-rangers/lore/cosmic-cascade/). Her most-quoted contribution to Cascade pedagogy is the sigh she gave one such questioner before answering: *"Where do I even begin?"* — a line the Survey Archive has since adopted, with her implied permission, as the honest opening of its own primer on the subject. See [Who Governs a Universe](/star-rangers/lore/who-governs-a-universe/).
