Isren Farrowkin has spent eleven local years arguing that the Federation assembly's weighted-vote formula undercounts biosphere-diverse worlds, and eleven local years losing that argument by a narrower margin each time. Colleagues who assumed the losing streak would wear her down have, one by one, stopped assuming it.

Isren's kinbeds run the deep chlorophyll green of a High-Reach Compact lineage, though she was bonded and raised in Understory Assembly territory — a split origin she has never let either Canopy Concord faction claim cleanly as one of their own, and treats as useful rather than awkward. She was seated as the Concord's senior Fourfold Accord delegate in 2819 UCSD, and has held Verdance's rotating Federation assembly seat, on and off between other member worlds' turns, since 2822 UCSD.

## Known History

- Bonded into the Farrowkin lineage in the Understory Assembly's territory, one of the last generations to complete kinbed-bonding before the Assembly restricted the practice to registered grove-elders following a contested case elsewhere in Concord territory.
- Trained as a cross-biome trade arbiter before entering Fourfold Accord service, handling disputes between Canopy Concord trade platforms and Fenmarch Republic shipping interests.
- Seated as senior Fourfold Accord delegate for the Canopy Concord, 2819 UCSD.
- First held Verdance's Federation assembly seat in 2822 UCSD; has returned to it twice since, most recently in 2826 UCSD.
- Sponsored the Federation's first formal data-sharing proposal with Star Ranger Survey Corps fold-route mapping, on the grounds that a species whose own biology depends on knowing exactly which light a given patch of ground receives has an unusually direct stake in accurate boundary-zone data.

## Character Notes

Isren argues from specifics, not principle, a habit colleagues attribute to her trade-arbitration years: she does not open with what is fair, she opens with what each side actually needs and what it would cost to get it. Federation delegates who expect Verdani coloration to signal a fixed faction loyalty are reliably wrong-footed by her — her Understory upbringing and High-Reach kinstrain give her genuine standing with both of the Canopy Concord's internal factions, and she uses that standing without much subtlety, one meeting for each side in the same week when the timing works.

She is direct about the Federation assembly's structural blind spot toward worlds like Verdance: a single seat for a planet whose internal politics run four independent governments deep, she has said more than once, treats Verdance's actual complexity as an administrative inconvenience rather than the thing that makes her world worth having at the table. She has not managed to change the formula. She has managed, by most other delegates' account, to make Verdance impossible to overlook regardless of what the formula says its seat is worth.

Off the assembly floor, Isren keeps her kinbeds in strict rotation between direct terrace light and the shaded intervals her Understory upbringing left her preferring — a small daily ritual colleagues have learned reads, reliably, as the closest thing to a tell she has.
