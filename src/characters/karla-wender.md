Karla Wender holds the rank of High Captain in the Star Rangers — strategic command authority above the Line Captains. A High Captain is responsible for operational doctrine, inter-agency coordination, and decisions that exceed the scope of individual line deployments.

Wender operates at the level where tactical outcomes become strategic commitments. She receives reports from Line Captains, including Tissadelle Shepherd, and translates field intelligence into command decisions. She does not micromanage lines; she defines the conditions under which lines operate.

She has a long service record and a precise understanding of the difference between what Rangers can be ordered to do and what they can actually survive doing. This understanding has made her cautious about orders she cannot personally defend.

## Known History

- Current posting: High Command, active duty.
- Command history: multiple prior theatres, details at High Captain clearance level.
- Relationship to Tissadelle Shepherd: direct command chain, high mutual confidence.

## Character Notes

Wender does not perform authority. She carries it, which is a different thing. Rangers who mistake quietness for uncertainty about her tend to correct that misreading quickly.

She has seen enough that she no longer requires situations to be explicable before she acts on them. She requires only that the action be the correct one given what is known. This distinguishes her from commanders who wait for certainty, and from those who act without it.
