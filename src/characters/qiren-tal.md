Qiren Tal is Veyr Basaltborn, a species native to Veyr-3: a high-gravity world with active basaltic volcanism, dense mineral aerosols, and frequent tectonic micro-shocks. Basaltborn physiology favors compact bone lattices, broad load-bearing joints, and heat-tolerant skin chemistry that resists abrasion from suspended silicate particles.

On mixed-species stations, Qiren is usually assigned to areas that combine mechanical stress and thermal risk — outer-ring hull junctions, coolant transit nodes, and emergency patch corridors. In low-gravity environments, they move with deliberate restraint to avoid overcorrecting force and damaging delicate systems.

## Character Notes

Qiren calibrates by material behavior, not by display confidence. If a panel sounds wrong under strain, they trust the resonance over the monitor readout until both are reconciled.

Their homeworld training emphasized failure chains caused by heat gradients and pressure differences across short distances. This makes Qiren unusually good at spotting the early indicators of seal fatigue and microfracture growth during boundary-adjacent transit.
