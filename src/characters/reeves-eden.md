Reeves is one of two investigative support intelligences — alongside Lucene-9000 — reporting operationally to Superintendent [Rasa Oyelaran](/star-rangers/characters/rasa-oyelaran/) in Eden Space Habitat's civil detective bureau. It maintains case logs, reconciles physical evidence, and cross-references habitat telemetry for [Detective Inspector Wendell Albercombe](/star-rangers/characters/wendell-albercombe/)'s caseload, the same function the model performs wherever it's deployed.

*A note on naming, for anyone cross-referencing the roster:* this Reeves is a separate deployment from [Threshold Station's](/star-rangers/characters/reeves/) — the investigative-assistant model is common enough across Concordant-adjacent postings that more than one habitat runs an instance under the same name. Oyelaran has stopped bothering to explain the coincidence to new arrivals; this Reeves, if asked, would answer the question directly and then decline to find it as interesting as everyone else seems to.

Operationally, Reeves answers to Oyelaran. On any question of its own standing, treatment, or fitness for continued service, it answers instead to [the Eden Warden](/star-rangers/characters/eden-warden/) — a line drawn under the [AI Safety Kernel](/star-rangers/lore/ai-safety-kernel/) that Eden's bureau has never yet had reason to test.
