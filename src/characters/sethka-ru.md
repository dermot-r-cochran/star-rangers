Sethka Ru is Serephine Dunekin, born on Kharis Belt IV: a low-humidity desert world with thin atmosphere, high UV flux, and severe thermal inversion between day and night cycles. Dunekin physiology emphasizes respiratory efficiency, light-scattering eye membranes, and water-conservative metabolism.

In dense-atmosphere habitats, Sethka wears lighter filtration gear mostly for comfort, not survival. He is at his best in sparse-signal environments where visibility is long, interference is low, and positional discipline matters more than speed.

## Character Notes

Sethka plans movement around exposure windows. He times routes by radiation profile, glare angle, and thermal shadow, then checks whether social plans can survive physical reality.

Because his homeworld punished small navigation errors with lethal consequences, he is precise about margins and skeptical of any mission brief that assumes stable conditions without measurement.
