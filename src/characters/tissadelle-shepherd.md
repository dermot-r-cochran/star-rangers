Tissadelle Shepherd holds the rank of Line Captain in the Star Rangers — the operational tier between field Rangers and High Command. A Line Captain commands a line of Rangers in active deployment and is responsible for mission continuity, resource allocation, and the safety of Rangers under her authority.

Shepherd is methodical under pressure and has a reputation for keeping her lines intact in environments where lines break. She does not volunteer information to superiors that has not been verified, and she does not accept unverified information from subordinates as actionable. This makes her slower than some commanders and significantly more reliable.

Her relationship to High Captain Wender is that of direct report and operational interface: Shepherd executes within the strategic scope Wender defines.

## Known History

- Current posting: active field command.
- Prior deployments: classified at Line Captain clearance level.
- No documented contact with Levrils or confirmed boundary events prior to current posting.

## Character Notes

Shepherd does not mythologise the Rangers or the role. She treats the title as a job description. This makes her unusually easy to work with and occasionally difficult to inspire — she is not moved by abstractions, only by clearly stated problems that have clearly available solutions.

She is aware that some situations do not have clearly available solutions. She handles this by continuing to work.
