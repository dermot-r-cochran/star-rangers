**RECOVERED RECORDING — AUDIO + TRANSCRIPTION**
**Performing credit:** Slipwave
**Recorded:** Eden Space Habitat, same season as the [Star Rangers Anthem](/star-rangers/codex/star-rangers-anthem/) recording
**Current standing:** Circulates less widely than the anthem. Not sung at musters — this one gets played, not performed, usually by someone marking a name that isn't coming back off a roster.

*Transcriber's note: the chorus and second verse below are confirmed against the recording itself — a listener's timestamped capture around the 1:27 mark matches them line for line. The rest of the song has not surfaced in any form we can verify, and is reconstructed here to fit around the confirmed lines rather than transcribed from audio. Treat the unmarked verses as best-guess scaffolding, not settled record.*
