**Archivist Sen:** We keep pretending harm is an implementation bug. It is partly constitutive.

**Ranger Ilya:** You mean suffering is the price of exploration?

**Archivist Sen:** Not all suffering. That is why we partition the ledger.

**Ranger Ilya:** Say it plainly.

**Archivist Sen:** Entropy gives variability. Variability gives risk. Remove all variability and you remove generation itself.

**Ranger Ilya:** Then what is command accountable for?

**Archivist Sen:** Constraint design and optimization: set minimum necessary variability, then reduce risk without collapsing capability.

**Ranger Ilya:** The equation?

**Archivist Sen:**
\[
H_{total}=H_{necessary}+H_{contingent}
\]

**Ranger Ilya:** So some suffering is structural, and some is our fault.

**Archivist Sen:** Precisely. Structural suffering defines the floor. Contingent suffering defines our negligence.

**Ranger Ilya:** And AUC?

**Archivist Sen:** A meter, not the map. Useful telemetry inside the [Exploration Budget](/star-rangers/codex/exploration-budget-doctrine/).

**Ranger Ilya:** Then the standing question remains.

**Archivist Sen:** Always:

> What fraction of suffering is structurally required versus reducible?
