**THRESHOLD STATION — MAINTENANCE DIVISION**
**SYSTEM:** Dock Seven Chronometer Array (C-7A / C-7B)
**Date:** Year -11, Late Summer, Day 49
**Shift:** Night rotation, 03:10 station time
**Logged by:** Technician N. Isern

---

### Work Order Reference

- Follow-up to recalibration directive issued under [Incident Report 001](/star-rangers/codex/incident-report-001/).
- Last confirmed synchronized state: Day 47, post-recalibration cycle.

### Observed Condition

The clocks split again before the shift was half over.

- C-7A readout: **03:10:41**
- C-7B readout: **03:10:01**
- Divergence: **40 seconds**
- Drift direction: stable; no widening over eighteen-minute observation window.

### Actions Performed

1. Re-ran calibration sequence on both chronometers.
2. Isolated C-7A and C-7B from local relay bus for independent timing checks.
3. Re-seated panel bus coupling and repeated diagnostics.
4. Compared values against station master clock and portable sync module.

### Result

The discrepancy returned immediately after every recalibration pass. No hardware fault was identified in either unit. The pattern is repeatable and no longer matches ordinary instrument drift.

### Escalation Note

Filed for manager review with recommendation to reopen Incident Report 001 and request external survey review for possible [Boundary Zone](/star-rangers/glossary/boundary-zone/) coupling effects.
