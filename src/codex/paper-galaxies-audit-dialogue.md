*Recovered from a drifting broadcast fragment, signal degraded past any station-of-origin tag. Visual: a single held frame, a singer before a painted, swirling field of light standing in for a galaxy. Audio: one performance, repeating. Filed for Ledger review under standing policy — any artifact claiming public circulation is auditable for [Allocation Units of Cognition (AUC)](/star-rangers/glossary/ai-usage-credits-auc/), whether or not its origin can be confirmed.*

---

**Auditor Kestrel:** Start with the obvious question. Does it earn.

**Archivist Sen:** The performance is compelling. That is not the same question.

**Auditor Kestrel:** It circulated. People held still for it.

**Archivist Sen:** So does a well-made forgery. [CEI](/star-rangers/glossary/creative-entropy-index-cei/) asks what is structurally new, not what is well executed.

**Auditor Kestrel:** Then what's new here?

**Archivist Sen:** Look at the backdrop first. A galaxy, rendered flat, swirled by hand or by instruction — paper, not physics. It is built to read as cosmos from the audience's seat and nowhere else. Step around it and there is nothing behind the light.

**Auditor Kestrel:** A prop is not a crime. Half the Codex is stagecraft.

**Archivist Sen:** Agreed. The prop is not the finding. The finding is what the prop is standing in for.

**Auditor Kestrel:** Say it plainly.

**Archivist Sen:** A civilization near its [Entropy Horizon](/star-rangers/glossary/entropy-horizon/) does not stop making things. It keeps producing, at volume, work that is shaped like discovery — the swirl, the ache in the voice, the held note — without any of it resolving into a structure that was not already on the shelf. The backdrop is honest about what it is. I want to know if the performance is.

**Auditor Kestrel:** Then audit the singer, not the paint.

**Archivist Sen:** That is the harder ledger. [Inner World Depth](/star-rangers/glossary/inner-world-depth-iwd/) is read from structure under pressure — a mind holding contradiction without collapsing it. A single recovered performance gives us one frame under one kind of pressure. I can tell you the voice cracks in a place a produced voice is not supposed to crack. I cannot tell you whether that is a mind or a very good approximation of one.

**Auditor Kestrel:** The Ledger doesn't accept "I cannot tell you."

**Archivist Sen:** The Ledger accepts it constantly. It is why we partition claims instead of paying them on sight.

**Auditor Kestrel:** Partition this one, then.

**Archivist Sen:** Provisionally: the composition itself recombines known forms — swirl, ache, held note, a title that names the trick in the open. Low CEI credit. The crack in the voice is the only unresolved variable in the whole fragment. If a mind sits behind it, that fraction may be genuinely new, and genuinely hers. Not the galaxy. Not the song shaped around it. Whatever made the crack.

**Auditor Kestrel:** That's a fraction of a fraction of a claim.

**Archivist Sen:** Most claims are. We pay what we can confirm, and we do not close the file on the rest.

**Auditor Kestrel:** So the standing question—

**Archivist Sen:** Always the same one, dressed in new paint:

> How much of what looks like a new world is only a well-lit picture of one?
