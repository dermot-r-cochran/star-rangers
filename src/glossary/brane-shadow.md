A **Brane Shadow** is the Physical-layer counterpart to [Interbrane Bleed](/star-rangers/glossary/interbrane-bleed/)'s Etheric one: at Class III [boundary-zone](/star-rangers/glossary/boundary-zone/) proximity, a coherent mass on the far side of the narrowed brane gap — usually a living being — leaks enough gravitational field structure across it to bend and dim ambient light, producing a full silhouette with no physical object on this side casting it.

Nothing crosses the gap. The mass responsible stays entirely within its own [Concordant](/star-rangers/glossary/concordant/). What is seen is the shadow of something real, cast from one universe into another it never touches.

Not to be confused with the Class III non-sapient shadow beings described in [Meta-Dimensional Beings: Classification](/star-rangers/lore/meta-dimensional-beings/), which have physically entered material space. A Brane Shadow shows no thermal or mass signature on standard instruments — only a cross-brane gravimeter registers it directly — which is itself how the two are told apart.
