**Levril** is the formal name for the entities commonly called dragons and their kin. They are the lowest named tier of the Cosmic Cascade — lower than Telerarchs and Frenar, but still more causally authoritative at their tier than any purely Material-layer entity.

Levrils are powerful and long-lived. They are bounded: they operate under constraints they cannot override, even though those constraints are at a higher tier than anything a Material-layer being could access.

**Meta-dragons** are a subset of Levrils that exist across boundary mismatches — they span more than one Concordant, paying a coherence cost for this.

Levrils may choose to mentor mortals in [constraint literacy](/star-rangers/glossary/constraint-literacy/). This is the closest thing to "teaching magic" that occurs in the Star Rangers universe, and it is emphatically not magic.
