The **Overfold** is what a [Fellowship of Light](/star-rangers/lore/fellowship-of-light/) contemplative chapter calls a boundary place: somewhere the world runs thin, patterns persist past their trigger, and a trained observer can feel a wrongness in the air long before an instrument confirms it.

It is not folklore standing in for ignorance. It is an old, careful name for the same lawful phenomenon that modern instrumentation calls [Etheric](/star-rangers/glossary/etheric/) interference and Survey Corps now grades as [Brane Resonance or Interbrane Bleed](/star-rangers/glossary/interbrane-bleed/) — coined centuries before any of those frameworks existed, by people whose only instrument was long, patient attention.

Three names, one thing, in the order they were coined: **the Overfold** (contemplative, oldest), **Etheric** (the formal cosmological layer the Five Layers model names), **Interbrane Bleed** (the current Survey Corps operational term). None of the three is more "real" than the others. Each names the part of the phenomenon its own tradition was positioned to notice first.

**Example:** Brother Daire, keeper of an old river abbey, never needed a chronometer to know a place was thinning. He had the Overfold, and centuries of the Fellowship's marginal notes on what it does to attention, sleep, and the reliability of small absences.
