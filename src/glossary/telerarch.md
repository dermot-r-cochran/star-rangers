**Telerarchs** are the ninth tier of the Cosmic Cascade — the enforcement layer that maintains the integrity of Concordant boundaries and ensures that Worldwright-level rules are not violated without consequence.

Telerarchs are not gods. They do not respond to prayer, perform miracles, or operate according to moral frameworks. They maintain structural integrity. When a rule is violated, the Telerarch-tier response enforces the consequence of that violation.

Many historical and in-universe religious traditions have misidentified Telerarch-level enforcement as divine intervention, divine punishment, or miraculous protection. This misclassification is understandable: the effects are real, they cannot be countered by Material-layer means, and they can appear to respond to morally significant events — because morally significant events at the Material layer often involve violations of Concordant-level structures.
