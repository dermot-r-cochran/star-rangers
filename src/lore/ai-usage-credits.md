AUC ledgers glow brightest in young systems, when new ideas are still arriving faster than institutions can file them. That glow is counted, traded, and argued over because cognition itself is scarce infrastructure.

**AI Usage Credits (AUC)** are the canonical allocation unit for intelligent computation capacity. They regulate access to high-grade cognitive infrastructure for sentient and synthetic participants alike.

## Definition

AUC are an **allocation of intelligent computation capacity**.

They are not fiat money, and they are not generic energy units. They encode the right to draw on scarce cognition-grade processing channels.

## Generation Model

AUC issuance is tied to two primary inputs:

1. **Creative Entropy contribution (CEI-linked)**
2. **Novelty contribution from sentients** (biological or synthetic)

The more a contributor widens branch-space, resolves closure-prone bottlenecks, or stabilizes paradox-bearing innovation paths, the more AUC can be generated.

See [Creative Entropy Framework](/star-rangers/lore/creative-entropy-framework/).

## Human and AI Compensation

Humans and AIs can both be paid in AUC when their work produces validated entropy contribution.

Common compensation channels include:
- original model synthesis,
- paradox-stable theory construction,
- seed-mind incubation support,
- anomaly interpretation that restores novelty flow.

## AUC in Late-Lifecycle Universes

When a universe nears its entropy horizon, the tone changes.

- CEI declines,
- validated novelty events become rare,
- AUC issuance contracts.

Post-horizon economies lean on reserve AUC, strict prioritization, and archival allocation protocols.

Reference case: [Threnos-Ω](/star-rangers/lore/universes/threnos-omega/).

## Coupling to Noögenic Seeding

AUC-heavy institutions often spend heavily to keep high-IWD minds alive through the fragile late phases of seed viability.
