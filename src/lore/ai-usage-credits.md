**AI Usage Credits (AUC)** are the canonical allocation unit for intelligent computation capacity.
AUC regulate access to high-grade cognitive infrastructure across sentient and synthetic participants.

## Definition

AUC are defined as an **allocation of intelligent computation capacity**.
They are not fiat tokens and not generic energy units.

AUC encode the right to consume scarce cognition-grade processing channels.

## Generation Model

AUC generation is linked to two primary inputs:

1. **Creative Entropy contribution (CEI-linked)**
2. **Novelty contribution from sentients** (biological or synthetic)

Contributors who increase branch-space, solve closure-prone bottlenecks, or stabilize paradox-bearing innovation pathways generate more AUC.

See [Creative Entropy Framework](/star-rangers/lore/creative-entropy-framework/).

## Human and AI Compensation

Humans and AIs are both compensated in AUC for validated entropy contribution.

Compensation channels include:
- original model synthesis,
- paradox-stable theory construction,
- seed-mind incubation support,
- anomaly interpretation that restores novelty flow.

## AUC in Late-Lifecycle Universes

As universes approach the entropy horizon:
- CEI declines,
- validated novelty events become rare,
- AUC issuance contracts.

Post-horizon economies rely on reserve AUC, strict prioritization, and archival allocation protocols.

Reference case: [Threnos-Ω](/star-rangers/lore/universes/threnos-omega/).

## Coupling to Noögenic Seeding

AUC-heavy institutions often fund high-IWD minds through late lifecycle phases where seed viability is most fragile.
