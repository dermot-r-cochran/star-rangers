Survey Corps analysts spent decades calling it "Etheric interference" and filing it next to instrument drift, because both showed up at the same stations for the same reason and nobody had a tighter word for either. The post-11 manifold gave them the physical geometry. It took longer to get the Etheric half of the picture into the same diagram.

## The Brane Model

The [Post-11 Dimensional Manifold](/star-rangers/lore/post-eleven-dimensional-manifold/) describes higher-order spacelike and timelike regimes beyond the first eleven spacetime dimensions — structure that governs cross-[Concordant](/star-rangers/glossary/concordant/) coherence, causality buffering, and fold survivability.

Within that manifold, each Concordant occupies its own bounded **brane**: a coherence zone with a fixed physics-plus-Etheric rule set, set by Worldwright design and separated from neighbouring Concordants by manifold distance rather than ordinary spatial distance. Under normal separation, one Concordant's [Etheric](/star-rangers/glossary/etheric/) archetypal templates have no measurable reach into an adjacent one. The two branes are real, adjacent, and functionally inert to each other.

[Boundary zones](/star-rangers/lore/boundary-zones/) are where that separation narrows.

## Two Effects, One Cause

The Post-11 Manifold entry already explains the *physical* half of boundary-zone behaviour: Dimensions 12–15 distribute geometric strain when branes overlap, and Dimensions 16–19 absorb the resulting timing disagreements — which is why two chronometers at a boundary station can each be locally correct and still disagree with each other.

The *Etheric* half runs through the same structure, one band higher: Dimensions 20+, the trans-concordant constraint field, is where each brane's archetypal templates are encoded. When brane separation narrows enough, that field lets one Concordant's templates become locally detectable — and, past a threshold, locally competitive — inside its neighbour.

Survey Corps now names this coupling **[Interbrane Bleed](/star-rangers/glossary/interbrane-bleed/)**, graded against the same Boundary-State Classes already used for the physical drift:

| Boundary-State Class | Physical signature | Etheric signature |
|---|---|---|
| Class I — Stable Gradient | Low-amplitude drift, predictable correction | Undetectable |
| Class II — Oscillatory Interface | Periodic chronometry divergence | **Brane Resonance** — detectable, non-displacing |
| Class III — Shear Event | Rapid discontinuity growth | **Interbrane Bleed** — neighbouring templates compete for local admissibility |

Neither signature causes the other. They are two observables of the same brane-proximity event, measured through different dimensional bands of the same manifold.

## Reframing the Threshold Station Record

This gives a mechanism-level reading of the [Dock Seven chronometer discrepancy](/star-rangers/codex/incident-report-001/): the Day 47 "calibration issue" closure explained the Physical-layer symptom, or tried to, and never addressed why the discrepancy [returned on Day 49](/star-rangers/codex/maintenance-log-day-49-dock-seven/) immediately after every recalibration.

A brane-proximity event does not resolve by recalibrating instruments on one side of it. The [Survey Corps Boundary Monitoring Protocol](/star-rangers/codex/survey-corps-protocol/) already required a certified Etheric-layer analyst for exactly this reason — Threshold Station's continuing lack of one is why the discrepancy kept being re-diagnosed as hardware rather than named as what the maintenance log's own escalation note already suspected: a boundary coupling effect.

## Operational Notes

- Brane Resonance is not hazardous and requires no intervention; it is background telemetry confirming a station sits near an active boundary.
- Interbrane Bleed is not supernatural and does not violate Cascade hierarchy — it is a lawful Etheric→Physical transfer under narrowed brane separation, consistent with the Five Layers' cross-layer coupling rule.
- No amount of Physical-layer recalibration resolves an Etheric-signature event. Etheric-layer certification exists specifically to read and respond to Brane Resonance before it develops into Interbrane Bleed.
