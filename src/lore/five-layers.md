Reality in the Star Rangers universe is structured in five layers. These are not physical strata — they are distinct modes of existence, each with its own rules of causation and expression. A being or phenomenon may be rooted in one layer and *express* through others.

**Critical rule:** The two foundational layers (Spiritual and Conceptual) are not characters. They do not speak, decide, intervene, or take sides. Any appearance of such intervention is either a misinterpretation or a lower-layer expression of higher-layer structure.

## Layer 1 — Spiritual

The origin layer. Beyond identity, beyond form, beyond any quality that can be named or perceived directly. It does not have a will. It does not communicate. It is not a god.

Attempts to make contact with the Spiritual layer are cosmologically confused: there is nothing to contact. What people misidentify as "spiritual contact" is almost always contact with Conceptual-layer coherence, Etheric-layer patterns, or the projections of their own Egregore constructs.

## Layer 2 — Conceptual

The layer of meaning, identity, and coherence — without embodiment. Ideas exist here before they acquire form. Entities that are primarily Conceptual in nature exist as coherence-patterns rather than as bodies: Court-Fae, Gilded Saints, Egregores, and Tulpas all root here.

The Conceptual layer is the feeding ground for predatory entities that harvest meaning, attention, or emotional labor.

## Layer 3 — Etheric

The law-bearing layer: proto-form, archetypal pattern. Where the Conceptual layer contains what a thing *means*, the Etheric layer contains the rules by which it *can exist*. Etheric structures are the templates that physical and material forms express.

When an "impossible" physical phenomenon occurs — an inconsistent physical constant, a strange resonance in solid matter, a pattern that persists after its physical cause is removed — this is typically an Etheric structure expressing through physical limitation.

## Layer 4 — Physical

Energy and spacetime. The domain of physics as conventionally understood. Forces, fields, particles, waves.

The apparent inconsistencies in physical constants observed near boundary zones are caused by interference between adjacent Concordants — regions of the Physical layer that have developed under slightly different Etheric conditions.

## Layer 5 — Material

Matter, biology, technology, and civilization. Everything that can be touched, built, bred, or destroyed. The layer where most story events are experienced.

Levrils, humans, Krenyi, robots, and elven kinds all live primarily in the Material layer, though beings with sufficient coherence (such as Krenyi) maintain more stable connections to deeper layers than beings with lower coherence (such as most humans most of the time).
