Teleportation in Star Rangers canon is not magic and not unconstrained instant movement.
It is a short-range harmonic relocation event that depends on the same lawful field behavior as **Quantum Space Harmonic Wave** transit.

## Core Limitation Set

Every legal teleportation event requires all of the following:

1. **Direct line of sight** between origin operator and target endpoint.
2. **Local quantum-space harmonic coherence** sufficient to hold a relocation lock.
3. **High available power reserve** to drive the relocation pulse.

If any one of these fails, teleportation must be treated as unsafe and non-executable.

## Why Line of Sight Is Mandatory

Teleportation lock is built from observed endpoint geometry, not inferred geometry.
Opaque barriers, heavy particulate fields, or active harmonic distortion break endpoint certainty and invalidate lock quality.

Operationally, this means:

- No legal blind jumps through walls or around corners.
- No legal jump to stale sensor-only coordinates without visual confirmation.
- No legal jump into boundary ambiguity zones without a verified visual lane.

## Physiological and Clinical Cost

Reported transit sensation is commonly described as similar to **electroconvulsive shock treatment**:
brief, disorienting, and neurologically intense even when the jump is technically successful.

Canonical medical risks increase with repeated or poorly timed teleport events:

- Elevated risk of **anxiety** symptoms.
- Elevated risk of **depression** symptoms.
- Increased likelihood of **central fatigue** and post-jump cognitive slowing.

These effects are measurable, cumulative, and require enforced recovery protocols in formal operations.

## Energy Economics

Teleportation is energetically expensive compared with ordinary local movement.
The relocation pulse requires a large short-duration power draw to force coherent matter-state remapping through the local harmonic field.

For this reason:

- Teleportation is reserved for high-value tactical or rescue cases.
- Stations throttle jump frequency to protect grid stability.
- Teams must budget power for life-support and shielding before authorizing teleport events.

## Continuity Constraints

- Teleportation always relies on quantum space harmonics; there is no separate mystical teleport mechanism.
- Stories may show disagreement about policy, but not about these physical constraints.
- Any chapter featuring teleport use must identify sightline status, energy state, and post-jump condition.
