Universe overlap and higher-dimensional folding are lawful geometry operations, not metaphysical exceptions.

Worldwrights create primary universes only. Secondary and subsequent universes are derived from primary ones through overlap and folding processes — they are not independently created.

## Primary and Derived Universes

### Definition
A **primary universe** is created directly by a Worldwright and contains one or more Concordants.

A **secondary universe** (and all subsequent derived universes) arises from the interaction of two or more primary universes through overlap or folding. Secondary universes are not independently authored; they inherit and recombine constraint sets from their source primaries.

### Constraints
- Only primary universes possess a Worldwright.
- Derived universes do not receive a new Worldwright assignment.
- Derived universes are structurally subordinate: their physics is a function of their source primaries' Concordant constraints.
- A derived universe that becomes stable enough to support Concordants does so under inherited, not original, Worldwright authority.

## Universe Overlap

### Definition
Temporary coexistence window in which adjacent Concordants share a measurable interaction domain.

### Mechanism
- Overlap begins when boundary phase displacement enters coupling range.
- Local physical constants remain position-dependent within the window.
- Overlap resolves when displacement exits coupling range.

### Constraints
- Overlap does not merge Concordants into one rule set.
- Overlap does not create new hierarchy tiers.
- Overlap cannot be permanently stabilised by Material-layer means.

### Observable Effects
- Dual-valid measurements from nearby instruments.
- Directional asymmetry in wave propagation and thermal behaviour.

## Higher-Dimensional Folding

### Definition
Localised topology rewrite of distance metrics while preserving global causality.

### Mechanism
- Fold scaffolds are generated using admissible higher-dimensional constraints.
- Translation proceeds only while scaffold integrity remains above threshold.
- Collapse sequence restores baseline topology.

### Constraints
- Folding is not equivalent to universe overlap.
- Folding cannot rewrite primary-universe Worldwright authority.
- Repeated folding in unstable boundary zones accumulates coherence debt.

### Observable Effects
- Spatial coincidence artefacts in optical and lidar systems.
- Time-bound cognitive desynchronisation near fold boundaries.

## Non-FTL Misuse Boundaries

- Overlap is not a substitute for propulsion.
- Folding is not licensed for retrocausal editing.
- Any procedure claiming moral attunement as a control input is invalid.

## Edge Cases

### Boundary-Proximal Folding
Fold operations near active boundary zones require stricter aperture limits.

### Overlap Without Transit
Overlap windows can occur without vehicle translation and still generate measurable drift.

### Cross-Book Continuity Safeguard
If two records conflict, prefer instrumented mechanism logs over witness moral framing.
