  <h1 class="lore-entry__title">The Outer Stations Consolidation Hearing</h1>
  <p><span class="character-badge">2719 UCSD</span></p>
</header>
<div class="lore-entry__body">

Scheduled for three weeks. It ran six months.

The Outer Stations Consolidation Hearing convened at Solar Command, Mars, in 2719 UCSD to assess the [Military Space Command](/star-rangers/lore/military-space-command/)'s operational adequacy across deep-range and boundary-proximate installations — a review forced, in large part, by the documented response-time failures around [Eden Space Habitat](/star-rangers/lore/eden-space-habitat/) and the unresolved disappearance of *Patience First*.

Eleven weeks into testimony, General Maren Solveig Krast answered a direct question about the MSC's slow response times with the honest version of her institution's defense, ending in a sentence she had not planned in advance: *"We tried democracy. The result was forty years of the Coherence Wars and three billion dead. I would rather be called a tyrant than repeat the experiment."* Three civilian observers recorded the address without the hearing chair's authorisation. It reached twenty-seven outer-station news services within forty-eight hours, and outran every substantive finding the hearing would go on to produce.

**Canon facts:**
- The hearing's formal finding — disputed by the MSC officer corps, endorsed by the civilian oversight panel — held that the MSC was structurally adequate for the conditions of 2650 but structurally inadequate for the conditions of 2719, and that the gap was widening.
- Specific findings included response-time inadequacy at fold-proximate installations, a boundary-zone knowledge deficit, and an accountability gap for local habitats and outer stations with no effective escalation path.
- General Krast's quote was taught inside MSC culture as defensible institutional memory; critics maintained the Coherence Wars were caused by democratic systems captured and destabilised by interests that could not survive real accountability, not by democracy in the abstract.
- The hearing's findings, and Krast's own quote, fed directly into the Consolidation Accords (2722 UCSD) and the Star Rangers Charter (2723 UCSD).
